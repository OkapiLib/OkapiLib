A simple structure representing the full ratio between motor and wheel.

The ratio is motor rotation : wheel rotation. So for example, if one motor rotation corresponds to two wheel rotations, the ratio is 1.0/2.0.

Parameters

iratiothe ratio of motor rotation to wheel rotation
